Automated Maintenance & Reminder Jobs

1. 100-Hour Inspection
   - Trigger: Aircraft reaches every 100 hours of Hobbs time (e.g., 100, 200, 300...).
   - Action: Automatically create a Maintenance Window and cancel overlapping reservations.
   - Frequency: Hourly check.

2. Annual Inspection
   - Trigger: 12 months since the last annual inspection date.
   - Action: Create a Maintenance Window and notify admins 30 days in advance.
   - Frequency: Daily check.

3. 50-Hour Oil Change
   - Trigger: Every 50 hours of Hobbs time.
   - Action: Create a short maintenance window or alert mechanics.
   - Frequency: Hourly check.

4. ELT Battery Replacement
   - Trigger: Expiration date of the ELT battery (usually every 12-24 months).
   - Action: Alert admins and create a maintenance window.
   - Frequency: Weekly check.

5. Transponder / Altimeter Certification (91.411 & 91.413)
   - Trigger: Every 24 calendar months.
   - Action: Alert admins to schedule certification.
   - Frequency: Monthly check.

6. Insurance Policy Renewal
   - Trigger: 30 days before the policy expiration date.
   - Action: Notify club administrators.
   - Frequency: Daily check.

7. Member Medical / BFR Expiry
   - Trigger: 30-60 days before a member's medical certificate or Biannual Flight Review (BFR) expires.
   - Action: Email the member and restrict their ability to create new reservations if expired.
   - Frequency: Daily check.
